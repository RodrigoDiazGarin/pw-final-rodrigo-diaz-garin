Awards

Introduction

The Incredibles (2004), received widespread critical acclaim, earning a total of 69 wins and 56 nominations across major award ceremonies. It was recognized in top-tier categories such as Best Animated Feature, Screenplay, Sound, Music, and Visual Effects. The film received multiple Oscar, BAFTA, Annie, and Grammy nominations and wins, showcasing its excellence in both creative and technical aspects. These accolades highlight the film's strong impact in the animation, storytelling, and production fields.
