Get to know the characters

Mr. Incredible

Once a legendary superhero, Mr. Incredible now works at an insurance company, feeling disconnected from his family and his past glory. But he'll soon discover that his greatest adventure is with them.

Elastigirl

Helen Parr has left her superhero days behind to focus on her family. Though she occasionally uses her powers at home, she's happy with a quiet life — and wishes Bob felt the same.

Dash

Dash is a ten-year-old with super-speed and endless energy. He struggles to hide his powers and doesn't understand why he shouldn't use them.

Violet

Violet is a shy, insecure teen who just wants to feel normal. Her powers — invisibility and force fields — reflect her desire to hide from the world.

Frozone

Lucius Best, also known as Frozone, can create ice from moisture and glide through the air on it. Though his hero days are behind him, he still supports Bob and brings his cool-headed style wherever he goes.

Edna

Edna Mode is a legendary fashion designer known for creating iconic superhero costumes. Though celebrated worldwide, she yearns for the return of Supers to once again blend cutting-edge technology with high fashion.

Syndrome

Syndrome is a brilliant but bitter villain who, despite lacking superpowers, uses his intelligence and technology to seek revenge on the Supers—especially Mr. Incredible, whom he once idolized.
